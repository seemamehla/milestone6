Positive effects of computer accessibility

Effects in school

  1. Computer accessibility plays a large role in the classroom. Accessible technology can enable personalized learning for all students.
  2. Students who can benefit from personalized learning
  3. In most classrooms, students can benefit from the following:
  4. Easier to see PCs for those who have a hard time seeing the board.
  5. Less cluttered PCs for those who have difficulty concentrating.
  6. Easier to hear PCs for those who have difficulty hearing the teacher speak.
